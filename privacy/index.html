Privacy Policy

Renew Technology Ltd  ·  Last updated April 2026

Introduction

Renew Technology Ltd ("we", "us", or "our") operates the ClipIt mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information You Provide

We may collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Email address (if you sign up for our newsletter or contact us)
  • Messages or feedback you send us

Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

  • Usage data (features used, session duration, in-app interactions)
  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • Crash reports and performance data

This data is collected to help us improve the App and is not used to identify you personally.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Respond to your enquiries and support requests
  • Send you updates and newsletters (where you have opted in)
  • Analyse usage trends to enhance user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Where Your Data is Stored

Renew Technology Ltd is based in the United Kingdom. Your data may be processed and stored in the UK or in other countries where our service providers operate. We take reasonable steps to ensure your data is handled securely wherever it is processed.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@clipit.it.

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services (such as analytics providers) that collect information as described in their own privacy policies. These services help us understand how the App is used and improve the experience for all users.

App Store Purchases

Subscriptions and in-app purchases are processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle your payment data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at support@clipit.it or via our contact page.
